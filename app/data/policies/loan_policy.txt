 LOAN APPROVAL POLICY

LendFlow AI approves loans automatically based on four underwriting criteria:

1. Credit Score: Applicant must have a credit score of 720 or higher.
2. Debt-to-Income Ratio: Applicant's debt-to-income ratio must be 35% or lower.
3. Annual Income: Applicant must have an annual income of at least $60,000.
4. Loan Amount: The requested loan amount must not exceed 5 times the applicant's monthly income.

If all four criteria are met, the loan is automatically approved.
If exactly one criterion is not met, the application is sent to manual review.
If two or more criteria are not met, the application is automatically rejected.

Approved loans receive a base interest rate of 8.0%. Applicants with a credit score of 780 or higher receive a 1.0% discount. Applicants with a debt-to-income ratio above 25% receive a 1.5% surcharge.
